# Publishable policy addition: AI-Powered Chart Image Analysis

**Do not publish this section until the active production provider chain and the policy URL are verified.**

## AI-Powered Chart Image Analysis

When you choose to use Trade Vision, you may select a chart image and ask YouTrader to analyze it. We send the image you select, its image type, your question, and limited aggregate trading metrics to an external AI-processing service through our secure service infrastructure so that it can provide the requested analysis.

Trade Vision is optional. Before the first image selection for a version of this disclosure, the app asks you to acknowledge that the image will leave your device. Please crop or remove personal, account, brokerage, or other sensitive information that may be visible in the image before you send it.

We do not use Trade Vision image content for Agent-007 analytics or general product analytics. The application does not write the source Trade Vision image to its database or local AI-response cache. The app may keep a local cache of the resulting analysis response to avoid repeat processing; you can delete that local Trade Vision cache in Settings. A source image remains on your device or in your photo library according to your device and photo-library settings unless you delete it there.

External AI services process the image under their own applicable terms and privacy notices. The active processors are listed in our subprocessors notice. We will state retention, training, and processing-location commitments only when they are verified for the active provider route and configuration. We do not use Trade Vision images for advertising or cross-app tracking.

To request information or ask a privacy question, contact `support@borovikgroup.com`.
